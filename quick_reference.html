🎵 Swara Reference

SaC · Shadja · Always shuddh
r / RC# / D · Komal/Shuddh Rishabh
g / GD# / E · Komal/Shuddh Gandhar
M / mF / F# · Shuddh/Tivra Madhyam
PaG · Pancham · Always shuddh
d / DG# / A · Komal/Shuddh Dhaivat
n / NA# / B · Komal/Shuddh Nishad
ṠC (upper) · Sa of next saptak
Sa and Pa are never komal — they are always shuddh (natural).
